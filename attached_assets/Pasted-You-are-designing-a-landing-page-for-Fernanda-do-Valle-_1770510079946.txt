You are designing a landing page for Fernanda do Valle Pessoa, a clinical psychologist, mental health author, and founder of the Instituto de Psicologia Positiva do Amazonas (Institute of Positive Psychology of Amazonas) in Brazil.

This is NOT a generic psychology clinic website, nor a simple institutional page, nor an isolated professional resume. This landing page communicates THREE INTEGRATED LAYERS of care:
1. Clinical Psychology (Individual psychotherapy)
2. Instituto de Psicologia Positiva do Amazonas (Protocols, projects, and education)
3. Books and Works in Mental Health (Accessible psychological content)

CORE CONCEPT: "Psicologia sob medida" (Tailored Psychology)
The central principle is that each person has a unique way of caring for themselves. The page must offer multiple pathways without hierarchizing them - clinic, institute, or books are equally valid entry points.

DESIGN AESTHETIC:
Follow the design.json attached as your complete design system guidelines. The overall aesthetic should feel:
- Calm and welcoming, never rushed
- Sophisticated and professional, yet warm and approachable  
- Scientifically credible without being cold or institutional
- Timeless and elegant, with generous white space that conveys TIME and LISTENING
- Natural and organic, using soft colors and rounded shapes
- Personalized and multilayered, visually communicating "customization" through subtle variations

VISUAL VALUES TO TRANSMIT:
- Time (not urgency)
- Listening (not noise)
- Science (not pseudoscience)
- Depth (not superficiality)
- Personalization (not standardization)

ETHICAL BOUNDARIES (CRITICAL):
❌ NEVER include:
- Promises of cure or guaranteed results
- Aggressive marketing language or artificial urgency
- Empty motivational phrases
- CTAs with pressure ("Don't miss this chance", "Limited spots")
- Generic stock photos of "self-care" clichés
- Overly edited or filtered imagery

✅ ALWAYS include:
- Scientific credibility (CRP registration, certifications)
- Clear, accessible, ethical language
- Multiple care pathways presented equally
- Respect for individual timing and needs
- Quality information over persuasion
